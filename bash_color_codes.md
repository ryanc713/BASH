# BASH Color Codes

Copy and Paste these into your .bashrc file.

````bash
export BLACK="\e[30m"
export RED="\e[31m"
export GREEN="\e[32m"
export BROWN="\e[33m"
export BLUE="\e[34m"
export PURPLE="\e[35m"
export CYAN="\e[36m"
export GRAY="\e[37m"
export END="\e[0m"

export BG_BLACK="\e[40m"
export BG_RED="\e[41m"
export BG_GREEN="\e[42m"
export BG_BROWN="\e[43m"
export BG_BLUE="\e[44m"
export BG_PURPLE="\e[45m"
export BG_CYAN="\e[46m"
export BG_GRAY="\e[47m"
````
